1. Generate content plan â†’ All articles get status "ğŸ“ Te doen"
2. Click "Schrijven" â†’ Status changes to "ğŸ”„ In progress"
3. Write article in editor
4. Mark as "ğŸ‘€ Review" when ready
5. After review, publish â†’ Status becomes "âœ… Gepubliceerd"

1. Find published article with status "âœ… Gepubliceerd"
